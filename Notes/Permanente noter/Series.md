If you have a sequence $\{a_n\}^\infty_{n=1}$ then the partial sum of the corresponding series is defined as
$$s_1 = a_1$$
$$s_2 = a_1 + a_2$$
$$\vdots$$
$$s_n = \sum_{i=1}^n a_i$$
This means that $\{s_n\}_{n=1}^\infty$ is the corresponding sequence of $\{a_n\}^\infty_{n=1}$.
